Our Story

How we met

Though Michelle and Alexander shared mutual friends, they never were introduced to one another. On one fateful night in October of 2011, Alexander sat in the frequented pizza shop Big Al's with his friends when he stumbled upon Michelle's Facebook profile. Intrigued by her scandalous profile picture, Alexander asked about Michelle and learned that his friends went to elementary school with her. That night, powerful words were spoken by Greg Mol to the effect: "You don't have a chance with her". Taking this as a personal challenge, Alexander added Michelle and eventually met with her in person. Love knew no time as they fell madly in love with each other...

The Engagement



August 10th, 2012

Knowing that this would be a once in a lifetime opportunity to propose to Michelle in front of all of her family just as she always dreamed, The young stallion Alexander fell to one knee proclaiming his love and intent to marry Michelle at their baby shower for Lillianna. With tears of joy, Michelle said yes. In September of 2012, Lillianna Lee was born. Several years later, Michelle gave birth to Kaiden Alexander in May of 2016.
